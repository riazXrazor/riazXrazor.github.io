ImgPos Plugin Demo

Original images

positions as percentage

            <img class="pos" data-posx="50%" data-posy="30%" data-height="150" data-width="150" src="img/2.jpg" />
            
            <script> 
                  $(function(){
                       $('img.pos').imgpos();
                  }); 
          </script>

ImgPos with just data-img-pos="true" no need to initilize with javascript
